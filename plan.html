My Plans

What I want to do with my capstone and my education.

For my capstone project, I'd currently like to focus on public transit and relate it to the environment. More specifically, I'd like study some of the negative aspects of public transit, such as increased air and noise pollution around transit centers and depots and come up with solutions to address those issues. I will probably focus on either Seattle or Tacoma, since they are local.

As for what I want to do after I graduate, I would be interested in working for a municipal government as an urban planner or something similar, as I do have an interest in devloping cities. I also would be interested in work revolving around environmental protection or conservation, such as with the parks.
